About Azure Wake

Private Zakynthos Cruises with Local Expertise

Azure Wake is built around the rhythm of Zakynthos itself. We design private boat experiences that connect guests with the island’s most admired scenery, from the towering white cliffs of Navagio to the glowing passages of the Blue Caves and the calm turtle waters near Marathonisi.

With experienced local skippers, flexible Zante itineraries, and premium hospitality on board, we turn a simple day trip into a polished island experience for couples, families, and private groups.
